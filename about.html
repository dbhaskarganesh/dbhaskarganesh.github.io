About

I am a Master's student in Computer Science at NLP2CT Lab, University of Macau. I am very honoured to be supervised by Prof. Derek F. Wong, an award-winning researcher who leads the Natural Language Processing & Portuguese-Chinese Machine Translation Research Group. I am passionate about machine translation and multilingual language models, with research experience in low-resource language processing and speech recognition systems for disordered speech.
