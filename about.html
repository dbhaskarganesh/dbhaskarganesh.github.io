About

I am pursuing an M.Sc. in Computer Science at the University of Macau, where I have the distinct honor of conducting research under the supervision of Associate Professor Derek F. Wong, an award-winning researcher who leads the Natural Language Processing & Portuguese-Chinese Machine Translation Research Group (NLP2CT Lab). As a dedicated Research Assistant in his lab, I contribute to cutting-edge work in low-resource machine translation and multilingual NLP systems.

Prof. Wong's recognition with the Macao Science and Technology Award (2022) and his leadership in deep learning applications for language processing have been tremendous sources of inspiration. Working alongside him has provided me with invaluable mentorship in developing production-grade NLP & Speech systems that deliver measurable impact in real-world scenarios, particularly in speech recognition for disordered speech and emotion detection.

My research focuses on advancing machine translation for low-resource language pairs, optimizing LLM frameworks, and creating accessible speech technologies that can bridge communication gaps for diverse user groups.

关于我

我目前正在澳门大学攻读计算机科学硕士学位，有幸在Derek F. Wong副教授的指导下开展研究工作。Wong教授是自然语言处理与葡中机器翻译研究组(NLP2CT实验室)的负责人，曾荣获澳门科学技术奖(2022年)，在自然语言处理和机器翻译领域成就卓著。作为他研究团队的研究助理，我致力于低资源机器翻译和多语言NLP系统的前沿研究工作。

Wong教授在深度学习语言处理应用方面的专业指导为我提供了宝贵的研究经验。在他的指导下，我专注于开发具有实际应用价值的NLP与语音系统，特别是在障碍语音识别和情感检测领域取得了显著成果。

我的研究重点是推进低资源语言对的机器翻译技术，优化大语言模型框架，并创建能够为多样化用户群体弥合沟通障碍的语音技术解决方案。
